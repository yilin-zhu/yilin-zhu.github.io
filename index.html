Hi! I'm Yilin Zhu.

I am interested in economic sociology and organizational sociology with an empirical focus on the emerging digital financial markets. Specifically, I research 1) how state and market actors interact to shape formal and informal institutions in the new financial markets; and 2) how novel forms of organizations in the new financial market emerge and develop.

I graduated from the University of Chicago in 2021 with a master's degree in sociology.
